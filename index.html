Catherine and Joe are getting married!

June 8th, 2013 in Cape Cod, MA

About Us

Catherine

Catherine is from Chicago and has lived in Boston for the past ten years. She is a transportation planner, working on local project in Boston, and loves hiking and exploring New England.

Joe

Joe is originally from Columbus, having lived in San Francisco and Paris before moving to Boston. He works in finance and machine learning, and does his best to surf, snowboard, and travel.

Their Story

Catherine and Joe met in 2005 at meetup in Boston. After a year of friendship and awkward flirting, they started dating, and after 6 years of awkward dating, they became engaged.

The got engaged during a hike at one of their favorite bird and wildlife sanctuaries.

Getting to Cape Cod

Airplane to Boston, MA or Providence, RI

Fly into Boston Logan airport or Providence T.F. Green. Both are about a 1.5 or 2 hour drive to Wellfleet.

Ferry from Boston to Provincetown

Take the ferry from Boston to Provincetown (you will need a car to get from P-town to Wellfleet).

Ferries operated by: Bay State Cruises and Boston Harbor Cruises .

Driving / Car Rental

Wellfleet (welcome party and brunch) is about a 1.5 to 2 hour drive from Boston. Truro (ceremony and reception) is about 20 minutes north of Wellfleet.

You can rent a car at the Boston or Providence airports, or in Provincetown, MA if you take the ferry from Boston.

We will have a shuttle from certain locations for the cermony and reception. See the accommodations page for more details.

Frequently Asked Questions


What is the dress code for the wedding?

Spring cocktail party attire (Men: suits optional, jackets suggested). Comfortable shoes (flats or wedges) and layers are recommended. The event will take place in a tented location with an uneven sand and gravel surface and it can get a little chilly near the water.

What is the schedule for the wedding day?

More details are coming, but the wedding will go from around 3pm to 10pm.

Does the reception really end at 10:00?

Truro and Wellfleet have strict noise ordinances. The music will end at 10:00 and the festivities will continue back in Wellfleet at the Pearl.

How do I get from my hotel to the wedding?

The venue has limited parking, so we are asking guests to use the shuttle bus between the Inn at Duck Creeke / Eventide Motel and the wedding. The shuttle will also be available after the afterparty at the Pearl. Please contact Joe or Catherine in advance to arrange a parking pass if you are not taking the shuttle.

Taxis in Wellfleet and Truro are very limited. Please either plan to take our shuttle or talk to us about driving / carpooling.

What will the weather be like?

It varies! Expect low 70s / upper 60s during the day, and low 60s / upper 50s at night. This is a rural area on the ocean, so bring layers, comfortable shoes, and a rain jacket (just in case!). Additional information about area weather: Weather Underground's Trip Planner/Seasonal averages.

Are children invited to the wedding?

The ceremony and reception are for adults only. Children are invited to the welcome party and brunch.

Do you have babysitter recommendations?

Email us and we can help you and the other families share a sitter or find your own.
